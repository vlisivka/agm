Market cup:
We didn't find any integrated solution for discussing and signing documents and contracts online that would be simple and comfortable for signing of documents. This means that there is a free niche in the market. We checked and found several potential customers who were interested in the idea, as it would save them money on travel and time for document sharing.
We checked the financial capacity of the market and found that in the Ukrainian market, one of the poorest in Europe, customers are ready to pay $20 per month. Also, some people are ready to pay for the filing and sending documents to official institution, up to $7 if it occurs once.
Sales and distribution:
According to estimates to reach the self-sufficiency we need around 2,000 businesses customers. From this cost we will pay for administrative work, programmers, lawyers, and security administrators for site. 2000 businesses can be founded just in one, single city with a population around a million peoples. Our low price does not include the cost of attracting users, so we focus on sharing information about us through the invitation system (for trusted people) as well as on direct experience through the casual signing of contracts or documents by single persons.
We plan to expand on the European market through Poland. The Polish and European markets are biggest than Ukrainian, so we hope that after the entry into the European market, we will soon increase capitalization. But this requires passing of all certification procedures to enter the B2B market what required investment and also we need an investment of around $ 150k to write the program code.
Space to Grow:
We can offer additional services for small businesses, such as primitive CRMs and ERPs, aimed at small businesses, but we don't know needs of the market about this, but this requires additional investment for programmers, so we will leave this for later at now. Also we can offer additional automation for big business, but for this we need to get state certification in each of the countries in which we will be work, which requires additional investment for lawyers, so we can not count on it now.
